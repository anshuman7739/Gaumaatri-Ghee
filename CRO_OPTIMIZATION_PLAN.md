## ✅ Success Metrics to Track

1. **Conversion Rate** - Orders / Visits
2. **Cart Abandonment** - Carts left / Carts created
3. **Payment Success Rate** - Successful payments / Payment attempts
4. **Average Order Value** - Total revenue / Orders
5. **Mobile Conversion Rate** - Mobile orders / Mobile visits
6. **Time to Purchase** - Average time from first visit to purchase
7. **Bounce Rate** - Single-page visits / Total visits
8. **Return Visitor Rate** - Repeat customers / Total customers

---

## 🎨 Design Principles

1. **Mobile First** - Optimize for mobile (70% of traffic)
2. **Clarity** - Clear CTAs and value propositions
3. **Trust** - Security badges, certifications, reviews
4. **Speed** - Fast page load (<3 seconds)
5. **Urgency** - Limited time/stock offers
6. **Social Proof** - Real customer reviews & testimonials
7. **Simplicity** - Minimal distractions, focus on conversion
